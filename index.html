Tamara

Tamara had always been passionate about food and when an opportunity arose to work in Canada making pizza, she took it. She quickly became adept at crafting delicious pizzas. Back in Ireland her passion for making pizzas didn't fade away. Inspired by the unique horsebox cafes, Tamara transformed an old horse box into a mobile pizzeria.

Two girls with notions came together and created an amazing mobile pizzeria in a horsebox!

Caroline

Caroline has a passion for marketing and a love for the hospitality industry. For 10 years, she dedicated herself to honing her skills, working at various hotels and restaurants.
